### Make sure all time frames are telling the same thing!

#### Base Breakout
A base where a rally in probable to happen.
#### Buy Set-up
Rally then a pullback that is 40-60% and weakening. Happens often after a base breakout.

#### 123 Pattern
One igniting bar then a rest bar during a base or a buy set-up.

#### The exhaustion play (For big corrections)
Five or more green bars in a row that have acceleration and extension from the 20 ma on multiple time frames.
