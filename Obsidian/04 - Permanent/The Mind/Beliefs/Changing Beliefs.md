1. Choose a belief.
2. Analyze and ask questions about this belief and its origins.
3. Replace it with another desired one with repetition and emotion.

The more emotion you generate, the faster the process will be.
You can use afformations: Record an audio of you asking questions about why you have the desired belief. Then listen it to it before after sleep.
You can also describe on a piece of paper with crystal clear details, an image of the version of yourself in the present tense.

Do this for a month.
